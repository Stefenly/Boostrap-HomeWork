Shop Overview

Private Sport + Outdoors brings together two different activities: design and production of sporting goods and direct retail to customers. Created in 1976 near Lille in northern France, Decathlon is now present in 25 countries, with 80,000 employees of 60 nationalities. Decathlon Cambodia was settled in Cambodia in 2011 with offices in Phnom Penh city. Our staff works closely every day with international customers and suppliers from all over Cambodia and neighboring countries to develop, industrialize, and supply sporting products. We are now developing the retail activity.

THE STARTING LINE

Summer 1976, it is on a parking lot of the Englos shopping center, near Lille, that everything begins. DECATHLON, his blue logo, his tent 2 Seconds ... In France, it's a bit of an institution. Who does not remember, little, afternoons spent having fun in the aisles of the shops ... Michel, Benoit, Didier, Stéphane, Hervé, Nicolas and Stanislas are sports buffs and entrepreneurs in the soul, although some do not know yet. Their plan - unpublished at the time: to equip in the same place and at the best price all the sportsmen, from the beginner to the enthusiast. They create Private Sport.
